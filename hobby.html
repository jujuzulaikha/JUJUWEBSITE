My Hobby


Baking

Since I was six years old, I have had a great passion for baking. I have made various types of desserts, with most of the recipes coming from my mother. My parents have always supported this hobby of mine by buying baking equipment for me.

Go to Concerts

Attending concerts is one of my favourite hobbies. Going to concerts makes me to appreciate music, especially local music from bands such as Hujan, Kugiran Masdo, Spider, and many others

Snorkelling

I am very fond of outdoor activities, and snorkelling is one of my top choices. This activity has truly amazed me with the beauty and wonder of marine life.
